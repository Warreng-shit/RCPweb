Main Dish


Mac&Cheese

Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 1 lb (454g) elbow macaroni
  • Butter: 1/2 cup (113g) unsalted butter, divided
  • Flour: 1/2 cup (60g) all-purpose flour
  • Milk: 3 cups whole milk, warmed
  • Cream: 1 cup heavy cream (or more milk)
  • Cheese: 10 oz sharp cheddar (shredded) + 10 oz Gruyere (shredded)
  • Seasonings: 3/4 tsp salt, 3/4 tsp black pepper, 1/4 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp mustard powder
  • Topping: 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs

Instructions

Preperation and Pasta
  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 3-quart baking dish.
  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook pasta for 1-2 minutes less than the package directions (it should be very firm/al dente).
  • Drain and toss with 2 tbsp of butter to prevent sticking, then set aside.
Make the Cheese Sauce (Mornay Sauce)
  • In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, melt 1/2 cup of butter over medium heat.
  • Whisk in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes until it smells nutty and is golden.
  • Gradually whisk in the milk and cream, stirring constantly to ensure no lumps form.
  • Cook, stirring regularly, for 5-8 minutes until the sauce has thickened to a cream-like consistency that coats the back of a spoon.
  • Remove from heat and stir in the salt, pepper, garlic powder, and mustard powder.
  • Stir in 3/4 of the cheese, 1-2 cups at a time, until melted and smooth.

Carbonara

Instructions

Ingredients
  • Pasta: 400g–1 lb (500g) Spaghetti, Rigatoni, or Bucatini.
  • Meat: 150g–200g Guanciale (cured pork jowl) or Pancetta, diced.
  • Cheese: 100g (approx. 1 cup) finely grated Pecorino Romano (or a 70/30 mix with Parmesan).
  • Eggs: 2 large whole eggs plus 2–3 large egg yolks (for extra richness).
  • Seasoning: A generous amount of freshly cracked black pepper; salt for pasta water.
Make the Cheese Sauce (Mornay Sauce)
  • Boil Pasta: Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add salt (it should taste like the sea) and cook pasta until al dente. Crucial: Reserve about 1 cup of starchy pasta water before draining.
  • Render Fat: While pasta cooks, place diced guanciale in a cold skillet over medium heat. Sauté until the fat renders and edges are crispy but not hard (about 5–7 minutes). Remove from heat.
  • Create "Crema": In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg yolks, grated cheese, and plenty of black pepper until a thick paste forms.
  • Temper the Eggs: Slowly whisk 2–3 tablespoons of hot pasta water into the egg/cheese mixture to "temper" it; this helps prevent the eggs from scrambling when they hit the hot pasta.
  • Emulsify: Add the hot, drained pasta to the skillet with the guanciale and its rendered fat. Turn off the heat or move the pan away from the burner. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta and toss vigorously for 1–2 minutes.
  • Adjust Consistency: If the sauce is too thick, add small splashes of the reserved pasta water until it becomes silky and glossy.

Filipino Spaghetti

Ingredients
  • Pasta: 400g–500g (1 lb) Spaghetti.
  • Meat: 250g–300g ground pork (or a mix of ground pork and beef).
  • Sausage: 4–5 Filipino red hotdogs, sliced diagonally.
  • Aromatics: 1 medium onion (finely chopped), 3–4 cloves garlic (minced).
  • Sauce Base: 1 cup tomato sauce + 1 cup banana ketchup.
  • Liquid: 1 cup tomato paste + 1–1½ cups water (or beef broth).
  • Vegetables (optional): ½ cup grated carrots, ½ cup diced bell peppers.
  • Seasoning: 2–3 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste), salt and black pepper.
  • Cheese: 1 cup grated quick-melt cheese or cheddar.
Cook the Filipino Spaghetti Sauce
  • Boil Pasta: Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add salt and cook spaghetti until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • Sauté Aromatics: In a large pan over medium heat, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant and translucent.
  • Cook Meat: Add ground pork and cook until browned. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
  • Add Hotdogs: Stir in sliced red hotdogs and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  • Build the Sauce: Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, banana ketchup, and water (or broth). Stir well to combine.
  • Add Vegetables: Mix in grated carrots and bell peppers (if using).
  • Sweeten: Add sugar and adjust according to your preferred sweetness. Simmer on low heat for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Combine: Toss the cooked spaghetti with the sauce until evenly coated, or serve sauce over pasta.
  • Finish with Cheese: Top generously with grated quick-melt cheese before serving.

Dessert

Leche Flan

Recipe

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 10 large
  • Condensed milk: 1 can (14 oz / 396g)
  • Evaporated milk: 1 can (12 oz / 354ml)
  • Sugar: 1 cup (for caramel)
  • Vanilla extract: 1 tsp

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Melt sugar in a pan over medium heat until golden brown, then pour into flan mold to coat bottom.
  • Whisk eggs, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla until smooth. Pour into mold over caramel.
  • Cover with aluminum foil and bake in a water bath (bain-marie) for 50-60 minutes.
  • Cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for 2-3 hours before serving.

Halo-Halo

Recipe

Ingredients

  • Shaved ice: 2 cups
  • Sweetened red beans: 1/4 cup
  • Sweetened jackfruit (langka): 1/4 cup
  • Sweetened banana (saba): 1/4 cup
  • Macapuno (coconut sport strings): 1/4 cup
  • Leche flan: 1 slice, cubed
  • Ube halaya: 2 tbsp
  • Evaporated milk: 1/4 cup
  • Ice cream (ube or vanilla): 1 scoop

Instructions

  • In a tall glass, layer the sweetened beans, jackfruit, banana, and macapuno.
  • Add cubed leche flan and ube halaya on top of the fruits.
  • Fill the glass with shaved ice, then pour evaporated milk over it.
  • Top with a scoop of ice cream and serve immediately.

Mango Graham

Recipe

Ingredients

  • Graham crackers: 200g, crushed
  • Condensed milk: 1 cup
  • All-purpose cream: 1 cup
  • Mangoes: 3 ripe, sliced

Instructions

  • Mix condensed milk and all-purpose cream in a bowl.
  • Layer crushed graham crackers, cream mixture, and mango slices in a serving dish.
  • Repeat layers until ingredients are used up, ending with mango on top.
  • Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving chilled.

Appetizer

Lumpiang Shanghai

Ingredients
  • 500g ground pork
  • 1 medium carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 small onion, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pack spring roll wrappers
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
Cooking Instructions
  • Mix meat, carrots, onion, garlic, egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  • Place a spoonful of mixture on wrapper, roll tightly, and seal edges with water.
  • Heat oil in a pan and deep-fry rolls until golden brown and crispy.
  • Drain excess oil on paper towels and serve with sweet chili sauce.

Kwek-Kwek

Ingredients
  • 12 quail eggs, boiled and peeled
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup water
  • Orange food coloring
  • Oil for deep frying
Cooking Instructions
  • Mix flour, cornstarch, baking powder, water, and food coloring into a smooth batter.
  • Dip boiled quail eggs into the batter, coating completely.
  • Heat oil and deep-fry eggs until golden brown.
  • Drain on paper towels and serve with spiced vinegar or chili sauce.

Chicharong Bulaklak

Ingredients
  • 500g pork mesentery or pork ruffles, cleaned
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
Cooking Instructions
  • Marinate pork in vinegar, garlic, salt, and pepper for at least 30 minutes.
  • Heat oil in a deep pan.
  • Deep-fry pork until crispy and golden brown.
  • Drain on paper towels and serve hot with vinegar dipping sauce.

Drinks

Ube Milk

Ingredients
  • 2 tablespoons ube halaya
  • 1 cup fresh milk
  • 1–2 tablespoons condensed milk
  • Ice cubes
  • ½ teaspoon ube extract (optional)
Preparation
  • Mix ube halaya and condensed milk until smooth.
  • Add ice cubes into a glass.
  • Pour fresh milk over the ice.
  • Add the ube mixture and stir well before serving.

Coffee Jelly

Ingredients
  • 2 cups strong brewed coffee
  • 1 sachet unflavored gelatin (7g)
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • Ice cubes
Preparation
  • Dissolve sugar in hot brewed coffee.
  • Add gelatin powder and stir until dissolved.
  • Pour into a container and refrigerate until firm.
  • Cut into cubes.
  • Place jelly cubes and ice in a glass, then pour evaporated milk.
  • Stir and serve cold.

Buko Pandan

Ingredients
  • 1 cup young coconut strips (buko)
  • 1 pack pandan-flavored gelatin, cubed
  • 1 cup nata de coco (optional)
  • 1 cup all-purpose cream
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • Ice cubes
Preparation
  • Prepare pandan gelatin according to package instructions and let it set.
  • Cut gelatin into cubes.
  • Combine coconut strips, gelatin cubes, and nata de coco in a bowl.
  • Add cream, condensed milk, and evaporated milk.
  • Mix well and chill before serving over ice.
